FAQ

Straight answers.

Is this just ChatGPT in a skin?
No. Apex Pulse PRO runs custom system instructions tuned for fitness, nutrition, and progressive training. It asks clarifying questions, remembers your goals between sessions, and returns precise programs with macros, sets, reps, and progression rules — not generic essay answers.
What happens to my data?
Conversations are processed by OpenAI under their privacy terms. We do not store your card data — Stripe handles all payments. Server logs are auto-deleted after 30 days. You retain full GDPR rights: request access, export, or deletion of your data at apexpulsepro@gmail.com.
Does it actually work?
It works if you follow it. Plans are realistic and adapt to what you'll actually do — not idealized protocols you'll abandon by week two. Most people who stick with the program see meaningful change in the first 30 days. Results depend on your consistency, not on us.
Is this a subscription?
No. One payment buys you 30 days. Nothing auto-renews. You don't need to remember to cancel. When the 30 days are up, you decide whether to renew — by paying again. That's the whole model.
Do I need a gym?
No. Most plans default to bodyweight or minimal equipment (resistance bands, a single kettlebell). If you have gym access, tell the coach in your first message and the program adjusts to use it.
Does it replace a doctor or dietitian?
No. The AI coach provides general training and nutrition guidance — it is not medical advice. If you have any health condition, are pregnant, nursing, or have a history of eating disorders, consult a qualified professional before starting any program.
