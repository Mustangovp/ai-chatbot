Questions

Straight answers.

How is this different from a generic chatbot?
APEX is built to reason about you. It asks what actually matters, remembers your goals and preferences between conversations, weighs your limits before it suggests anything, and is designed to keep your safety first. It's personal coaching guidance — not one-size-fits-all answers.
Is it safe to follow?
APEX gives general training and nutrition guidance for healthy adults and is built to prioritise your wellbeing. It is not medical advice. If you have a health condition, take medication, are pregnant or nursing, are under 18, or have a history of an eating disorder, please talk to a qualified professional before starting anything new.
Does it replace a doctor, dietitian, or coach?
No. APEX is guidance and education — it's built to work alongside the professionals in your life, never to replace them. For diagnosis, treatment, or anything medical, always see a qualified professional.
Will I actually stick with it?
That's the part APEX is built for. Instead of an idealised plan you abandon by week two, it shapes guidance around what fits your real week and adjusts as life shifts. Consistency comes from guidance you can actually follow — your effort still does the work.
Is this a subscription?
No. One payment covers 30 days, and nothing auto-renews. You never have to remember to cancel. When the 30 days end, you decide whether to continue — by choosing to pay again. That's the whole model.
Do I need a gym?
No. Guidance defaults to bodyweight or minimal equipment. If you do have gym access, mention it in your first message and APEX will factor it in.
What happens to my data?
Conversations are processed by our AI provider under their privacy terms. We don't store your card details — Stripe handles payments. Server logs are auto-deleted after 30 days, and you keep full GDPR rights to access, export, or delete your data at apexpulsepro@gmail.com.
