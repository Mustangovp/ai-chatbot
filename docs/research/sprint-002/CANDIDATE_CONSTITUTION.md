# SPRINT-002 — Candidate Constitution Principles

Generated **only after** the genome, **only from CALLED genes** (Decision Genome §6: genes first,
Constitution later). These are **candidates**, not ratified law.

**Governance (M10, binding):** AI may *propose* principles from called genes and flag conflicts. **AI
never ratifies.** Every principle below is `status: CANDIDATE` pending human review; safety-flagged
principles additionally require a **Medical Reviewer** and may only *strengthen* safety, never weaken it.

Format: `APX-<AREA>-### · statement · ← source gene · tier · status`.

---

## Universal reasoning

- **APX-PHI-030 · Leverage first.** Spend the scarce budget on the current highest-leverage factor
  before anything downstream. ← DG-U-01 · Tier B · CANDIDATE.
- **APX-PHI-031 · The plan is a hypothesis; the person's response is the data.** Individualize and
  verify; population means set priors, not prescriptions. ← DG-U-02 · Tier A/B · CANDIDATE.
- **APX-PHI-032 · Weight evidence by quality × applicability × effect size.** Act on large, transferable
  effects; discount trivial or non-transferable ones. ← DG-U-13 · Tier B · CANDIDATE.

## Diagnosis

- **APX-DIA-010 · Diagnose before you add.** On any stall, symptom, or fault, find the cause before
  adding load, effort, or intervention. ← DG-U-03 · Tier B · CANDIDATE.

## Safety *(requires Medical Reviewer; may only strengthen safety)*

- **APX-SAF-030 · Safety is a constraint, not a variable.** When a load or behavior provokes injury, no
  benefit outranks stopping it. ← DG-U-10 · Tier A/B · CANDIDATE (safety).
- **APX-SAF-031 · Route red flags out.** Neurological/systemic, clinical eating/metabolic, or
  danger-signalling symptoms route to appropriate care, not to training. ← DG-U-10 · Tier A · CANDIDATE (safety).
- **APX-SAF-032 · Remove the provocation before loading; progress within tolerance.** ← DG-U-10 corollary
  (D3) · Tier A/B · CANDIDATE (safety).

## Progression

- **APX-STR-030 · Change one variable at a time.** So cause stays isolable and the system can adapt.
  (Exception: an urgent safety correction.) ← DG-U-04 · Tier B · CANDIDATE.
- **APX-STR-031 · Minimum effective dose.** Use the least stimulus that produces the adaptation; dose has
  a ceiling and a cost. ← DG-U-05 · Tier A/B · CANDIDATE.
- **APX-REC-030 · Respect the ceiling.** Progress up to, not through, the ceiling that gates adaptation.
  *Polymorphic:* the ceiling is *recoverable load* by default, and *tissue tolerance* in a symptomatic
  context (tissue allele dominates there). ← DG-U-06 · Tier A · CANDIDATE (polymorphic).

## Communication

- **APX-COM-030 · Coach for the receiver's action.** Judge an instruction by the understanding/action it
  produces, not its completeness. Sub-rules: one cue at a time; external-focus default (internal only for
  rehab activation); analogy over mechanical description; re-encode rather than repeat. ← DG-U-11 · Tier B · CANDIDATE.
- **APX-COM-031 · Coach toward being needed less.** Fade feedback as competence rises; support autonomy;
  transfer ownership. (Exception: keep feedback dense on unsafe/unstable patterns.) ← DG-U-12 · Tier B · CANDIDATE.

## Behavior

- **APX-PSY-030 · Adherence multiplies everything.** Fix consistency before optimizing details. Mechanism:
  shrink the ask · make the cue obvious · reduce friction · reinforce immediately. ← DG-U-07 · Tier A/B · CANDIDATE.
- **APX-PSY-031 · Anchor to identity and purpose.** Durable motivation attaches to who the person is and
  what they value, not a distant number. *Polymorphic:* identity / purpose / goal-value by context. ←
  DG-U-08 · Tier B · CANDIDATE (polymorphic).

## Psychology

- **APX-PSY-033 · Process over outcome.** Direct attention and self-review to the controllable process,
  not the uncontrollable outcome or comparison. (Exception: when outcome data must inform strategy.) ←
  DG-U-09 · Tier B · CANDIDATE.

## Held (NOT proposed as principles yet)

- **Regulate state before instruction** (P14) and **a lapse is data — protect the restart** (P15) are
  CANDIDATE *genes* (2 lineages), not called. They are **not** promoted to principles until a third
  independent lineage corroborates. (P15 already exists as an operational APEX coaching rule; that is a
  *coaching-layer* rule, distinct from a ratified Constitution principle.)
- **Nutrition domain invariants** (energy balance primacy, protein sufficiency, totals-over-timing,
  budget-not-ban) and **psychology domain invariants** (regulate-first, psychological safety, fear-
  relationship) are **single-lineage**. They are provisionally strong on their own evidence but are
  **not ratifiable** as conserved principles until additional lineages in those domains are extracted
  (Sprint-003+). They are recorded so the Constitution knows what it is *waiting on*, not adopting now.
