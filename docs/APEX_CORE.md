# APEX Core: Visual Identity & Evolution Guidelines

This document establishes the official specifications and freeze guidelines for **Living Core Version 1.0**, the permanent face of APEX.

---

## 1. Purpose
The Living Core is the primary visual channel through which the user perceives the internal state of APEX. It is not an ornamental widget or a temporary visual effect. It is the somatic representation of the system’s reasoning and attention.

---

## 2. Visual Identity Rules
The Living Core must conform strictly to the following parameters:
- **Geometry**: Concentric circular orbital ring paths carrying flowing particle vectors.
- **States**: The Core operates strictly in one of five states:
  1. `IDLE`: Calm biological breathing cycle with cyan recovery glow.
  2. `LISTENING`: Pauses breathing cycles; orbits and particles accelerate dynamically in sync with user audio bounds.
  3. `PROCESSING`: Inner orb contracts; slows rotation into a calm Amber/White palette.
  4. `SPEAKING`: Expands and pulses rhythmically with vocal amplitude peaks; emits expanding ripples at sentence bounds.
  5. `SAFETY`: Rotates to a standstill; locks into a static, deep Red safety halo.

---

## 3. Evolution Rules
While future iterations may improve rendering pipelines, shaders, WebGL performance, physics viscosity, or responsive frame targets, the following rules govern all updates:
- **Structural Legibility**: The concentric rings, nucleus node, and orbiting particles must remain clearly recognizable.
- **State Mappings**: The color schemes, scales, and biological frequencies of the five states are frozen and cannot be changed or remapped.
- **Performance**: The rendering loop must maintain 60 FPS across desktop and mobile form factors.

---

## 4. Never Replace Policy
- **No Replacement**: The Living Core cannot be swapped for human avatars, cartoon characters, marketing overlays, or different graphical layouts.
- **V1.0 Frozen**: The visual identity defined in this version is part of the permanent APEX brand identity.
